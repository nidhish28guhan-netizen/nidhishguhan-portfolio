Founder & Inventor — Startup Plan

BizPrime: one platform to run any business, on any device.

BizPrime is my own startup venture — a complete business management platform I designed and am building from the ground up, spanning two dedicated products: one for mobile, one for desktop.

Mobile

BizPrime Flow

The mobile experience of BizPrime — built for managing business on the go.

Desktop

BizPrime Business OS

The desktop counterpart — a full operating environment for running a business.

BAE

BizPrime Adaptive Engine

At the core of BizPrime is BAE — the BizPrime Adaptive Engine — a technology of my own invention. BAE runs on a single, unified database shared across both the mobile and desktop products, and is built to adapt to different types of businesses within one piece of software — rather than requiring separate systems for each.
